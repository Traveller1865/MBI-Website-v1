Competitive Landscape

Forward Health

Model: Membership-based preventive care with high-tech clinics
Price: ~$149/month ($1,800/year)
Strengths: Premium experience, no wait times, data-driven
Weaknesses: High price, limited reach, capital-intensive

Parsley Health

Model: Holistic functional medicine with lifestyle coaching
Price: ~$150/month ($1,800/year)
Strengths: Holistic approach, telehealth options, personalized
Weaknesses: Price barrier, physical centers, scaling challenges

MBI (Our Approach)

Model: AI-powered preventative platform with tiered membership
Price: $99-349/month (tiered approach)
Strengths: Integrated ecosystem, AI personalization, scalable
Differentiation: Insurance pathway, multiple revenue streams
